## Trend 1: AI Anxiety

In 2026, AI is no longer a "nice to have" — it's a survival threshold.

**Big Tech:** After three rounds of restructuring, the core theme is "AI-first." Non-AI project budgets are being cut across the board.

**Startups:** AI-native companies command premium valuations. Non-AI startups are seeing 20-30% valuation discounts.

**The middle class of enterprises:** Most anxious group. They know they need to transform but fear the cost and risk.
