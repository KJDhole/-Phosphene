## Film: The Rise of Content Power

Douban's new film "Obsession" (痴迷) opened with a 7.7 rating — not a blockbuster score, but a signal. It represents a shift: **content quality is overtaking star power.**

Three trends in 2026 Chinese cinema:
1. Genre maturity — suspense, sci-fi, and realistic dramas all have dedicated audiences
2. Word-of-mouth long tail — good films find their audience slowly
3. Short video re-creation — platforms like Douyin are becoming new marketing channels

## Variety Shows: Back to Basics

Music shows are shifting from competition to creation. Slow-paced, "healing" variety shows are the new trend. Stand-up comedy is booming offline, with online shows serving as funnel entry points.

## Short Dramas: The Third Pole

Short-form dramas are going premium. Per-episode production costs have risen from 10K RMB to 100K+. The overseas market for Chinese short dramas is exploding.

## Three Predictions

1. **AI + Entertainment** — AI writing, AI short dramas, AI music will become new content supply
2. **Premium short dramas** — no longer seen as "low quality"
3. **Emotional value** — audiences pay for "resonance," not just "spectacle"
